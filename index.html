Gaia Glamping

A sustainable retreat in the heart of Surrey

Feel at home

Gaia Glamping is nestled away in the heart of the Surrey Hills. With nobody to bother you except the birds, it's a place to hideaway and relax. Our beautiful two bedroom space allows you to connect with nature, but with all of the luxuries of home.

Our space was hand built with local timber and our furniture has been sourced from vintage stores across the UK. We love to use natural materials wherever possible. Our bath and shower products are from local business Naturalee, who specialise in working with eco-friendly suppliers. Our cleaning products are by Smol, and you'll find some local coffee by our friends at Beany waiting for you upon arrival.

Outdoor Paradise

Gaia's private outdoor area features a stunning roll-top bathtub, an outdoor shower and hanging rope chair. We also have a well-equipped outdoor cooking area with high-top seating and separate bbq pit.

The view from the deck is absolutely breathtaking. It's easy to sit and while the hours away just taking it all in! Our outdoor area is the perfect spot to be 'at one' with nature, whether you're cooking, reading a book or simply enjoying a morning coffee.

Cosy spaces

Our spaces have been designed thoughtfully and intentionally. Each room has a cosy feel, with linen bedsheets and lots of blankets to keep you warm.

We have a custom fitted wood-burner which not only looks gorgeous, but will keep you toasty on those chilly evenings.
